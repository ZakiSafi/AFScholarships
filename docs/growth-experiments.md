# 90-Day Growth Experiments

## Experiment 1: SEO Topic Clusters
- Goal: capture high-intent scholarship search traffic.
- Tactic: publish weekly pages for scholarship terms relevant to Afghan students.
- Success metric: organic signup conversion rate.

## Experiment 2: Deadline Alerts on Social Channels
- Goal: increase weekly active and returning users.
- Tactic: publish weekly deadline alerts on Facebook, Instagram, Telegram, and LinkedIn.
- Success metric: weekly returning users and click-through to detail pages.

## Experiment 3: Campus and NGO Partnerships
- Goal: acquire high-quality activated users.
- Tactic: partner with universities, institutes, and NGOs that advise students.
- Success metric: activation rate (`save scholarship`) and partner referral signup volume.
